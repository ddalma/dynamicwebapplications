About me

I am a Professor of Mathematics at Polytechnic Institute Of New York University. Not only do I teach mathematics, I am a researcher as well, with concentrations/specializations in areas such as convex geometry.I believe mathematics plays an important role not only in engineering but it also builds critical thinking skills applicable in many sorts of fields.
